# Anomaly Alert Template

## Structure

### What Happened
- Metric, channel, segment affected
- Current value vs baseline (trailing 8-week avg)
- Z-score

### How Big
- Absolute delta in USD or volume
- Percentage change
- Estimated weekly/monthly impact if trend continues

### Why (Hypothesis)
- Top hypothesis with confidence level
- Supporting and contradicting evidence

### What To Do
- Recommended immediate action
- ICE score

### Kill Criteria
- At what point do we escalate or reverse course
- Monitoring cadence (daily until resolved)
